SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into on January 15, 2025, between:
- AlphaTech Solutions Inc. (“Client”)
- NovaBuild Software LLC (“Contractor”)

1. SCOPE OF WORK
The Contractor agrees to design and deploy a custom web dashboard for the Client’s internal analytics system.  
Work shall commence within five (5) business days after the date of this Agreement.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
The total project cost is $120,000 (USD), divided into the following milestones:
   - Milestone 1: $30,000 due upon completion of the wireframe prototype, expected within 15 days of project start.
   - Milestone 2: $60,000 due upon delivery of the fully functional beta version, expected within 45 days of project start.
   - Milestone 3: $30,000 due upon final acceptance and deployment, within 10 days of delivery.

All payments shall be made via escrow and released upon Client approval of each milestone.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree not to disclose or share any confidential information.  
This obligation remains in effect for a period of two (2) years after contract termination.

4. DELAYS AND PENALTIES
If the Contractor fails to meet any delivery milestone by more than seven (7) days, a late penalty of 2% of the milestone value will apply per week of delay.  
If delay exceeds 30 days, the Client may terminate the Agreement.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing 15 days’ written notice.  
Upon termination, all work completed shall be paid proportionally to the effort delivered.

6. WARRANTIES
The Contractor warrants that the deliverables will be free from material defects for a period of ninety (90) days after final acceptance.  
Any defects reported during this period must be resolved within ten (10) business days.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
